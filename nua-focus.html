Design Philosophy

Small space,
generous spirit.

The Nua Focus is shaped around a single principle: that a small space, when properly considered, should feel generous. A continuous curved profile, full-height glazing and a panoramic balcony extend the interior outward, while a restrained palette of warm timber tones, brushed metals and soft ambient lighting establish a calm, residential atmosphere within.

The pod is engineered as a sealed architectural envelope — quiet, weather-tight and thermally stable for the Irish climate. It arrives complete: structure, services, fixtures, finishes and smart systems pre-installed, commissioned and ready for connection on site.

Principal Features

Engineered
without compromise.

Precision-engineered structural shell with hot-dip galvanised steel chassis and aviation-grade aluminium cladding finished in matte fluorocarbon.

Three-layer thermal envelope with high-density polyurethane insulation, achieving year-round comfort with minimal energy demand.

Full-height double-glazed Low-E curtain wall with thermal-break framing — exceptional clarity, acoustic isolation and solar control.

Integrated smart-home system with voice command and a centralised control panel governing lighting, climate, audio and motorised drapery.

Optional underfloor heating, antifreeze package and illuminated starlit ceiling for cooler climates and immersive interiors.
