Design Philosophy

Open plan,
generously resolved.

The Nua Nest is shaped around a single principle: that a small space, when properly considered, should feel generous. A long, open-plan volume with full-height panoramic glazing along its length draws the landscape inward, while a restrained palette of warm tones, brushed metals and soft ambient lighting establish a calm, residential atmosphere within.

The pod arrives complete — structure, services, fixtures, finishes and smart systems pre-installed, commissioned and ready for connection on site.

Principal Features

A complete home,
ready from day one.

Precision-engineered structural shell with reinforced seismic-rated steel chassis and aviation-grade aluminium cladding finished in matte fluorocarbon.

Three-layer thermal envelope with 100 mm combined high-density polyurethane insulation — year-round comfort with minimal energy demand.

Full-height panoramic curtain wall with double-tempered Low-E glazing and thermal-break framing — acoustic isolation and solar control.

Fully fitted en-suite bathroom with smart bidet-toilet, multi-function bath unit and integrated overhead shower — finished to a boutique hotel standard.

Integrated smart-pod system with voice command and a centralised control panel governing lighting, climate, audio and motorised drapery.

Optional fitted kitchen, electric underfloor heating, projector with motorised screen and antifreeze package.
